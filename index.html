What's New

  • Bus

    Everyday after school all the students who are going by bus come down to the lobby to board their bus which will take them home. To identify which bus to in there are different types of busses which will go to different areas, for example 'P-M 3'. Also while going to places such as the P.E ground or field trips everybody takes the school bus.

  • Online Classes

    Ever since the pandemic all the schools have been shut down. Right now RN Podar School is currently doing classes online with google meet. At the beginning they only took classes once a week but soon when everybody got used to it they started doing it daily. Due to the inernet we can't take classes for 4 hours as they did physically so the school kept on shifting the timings. Currently the timings for grade 4 is 2 hours 30 minutes including a 30 minutes break. The benefit of online classes are that we can take them from anywhere just with a device either it be a phone, tablet or laptop. Since we are doing classes from home the people have decidedto renovate the building to a new one.
